# TherapyNote - Marketing & Launch Strategy

## Go-to-Market Overview

TherapyNote targets a niche, professional market. Success depends on reaching therapists where they congregate, demonstrating clear ROI, and leveraging word-of-mouth.

**GTM Pillars:**
1. **Product Hunt launch** (Week 12) - Drive initial awareness + users
2. **Therapy community outreach** - LinkedIn, Reddit, therapy forums
3. **Word-of-mouth & referrals** - Therapists tell 3-5 colleagues (tight community)
4. **Content marketing** - Blogs, case studies, ROI calculators
5. **Strategic partnerships** - Integration with therapy practice networks

---

## Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-11)

### Building Credibility & Social Proof

**Week 1-5: Stealth Mode**
- Build in public (Twitter/LinkedIn updates 2x/week)
- Share development progress (transparency builds trust)
- Engage with therapy/mental health community
- Follow thought leaders in digital health, therapy tech

**Week 6-8: Beta Community**
- Recruit 10-15 beta testers from network
- Create private Slack/Discord for beta feedback
- Weekly check-ins with beta users
- Document feedback and iteration
- Prepare testimonial video with 2-3 beta users

**Week 9-11: Social Proof Building**
- Publish 1-2 blog posts (therapy burnout, documentation, AI)
- Create case study with beta users (before/after time savings)
- Record tutorial videos (3-5 minute walkthrough)
- Build email list (landing page signup → 50-100 early interest)
- Prepare Product Hunt page (screenshots, description, pricing)

---

## Launch Week Strategy (Week 12)

### Product Hunt Launch Day

**Pre-Launch (24 hours before)**
- Schedule Product Hunt page to "coming soon"
- Email beta users to ask them to upvote on launch day
- Post on Twitter about tomorrow's launch
- Tag Product Hunt in posts, invite press

**Launch Day (Tuesday 9 AM PT)**
- Post on Product Hunt
- Launch email campaign to 50-100 interested users
- Tweet thread explaining the problem + solution (thread of 8-10 tweets)
- Comment on Product Hunt comments every 15 min (respond to questions)
- Share screenshot + short video on LinkedIn
- Engage with other Product Hunt comments (upvote helpful comments)
- Monitor analytics dashboard (signups, conversion rate)

**Launch Week (Days 2-7)**
- Answer Product Hunt comments daily (hours 2-12 after launch)
- Use Product Hunt API to track conversation sentiment
- Respond to email inquiries personally (first 50 users)
- Feature 1-2 beta user testimonials in comments
- Share user success stories on Twitter

**Expected Outcomes:**
- 200-500 signups from Product Hunt
- 20-30 conversions to paid ($1-2K MRR)
- 1-2 press mentions
- 500-1K Twitter impressions
- Credibility/social proof for next phase

## Parallel Launch Channels (Week 12)

### 1. Therapy Community Forums & Groups

**Targets:**
- Reddit: r/therapists, r/psychotherapy, r/mentalhealth
- Psychology Today forum
- AAMFT (American Association for Marriage & Family Therapy) groups
- State-specific therapy licensing boards (Facebook groups)
- BetterHelp/Talkspace communities

**Approach:**
- Post value (don't spam) - offer free 3 months to first 20 who sign up
- Share case study: "Saved me 8 hours/week on documentation"
- Answer questions authentically
- Provide ROI calculator ("This saves you $X/month")

**Effort:** 2-3 hours/week management
**Expected Users:** 20-50 from communities

---

### 2. Twitter/LinkedIn Outreach

**Twitter Strategy:**
- Tweet daily (1 tweet/day about therapy burnout, AI, productivity)
- Share blog posts, case studies, user testimonials
- Engage with therapy/healthcare Twitter community
- Use hashtags: #therapy #mentalhealth #burnout #AI #healthcare

**LinkedIn Strategy:**
- Post 2-3x/week (longer-form articles)
- Target therapy practice owners, clinical directors
- Share customer success stories
- Run ads to therapy practice managers ($50-100/week budget)

**Expected Users:** 30-50 from social channels

**Campaign 2: Launch Day**

- CTA: Sign up for free trial

**Campaign 3: Case Study (Week 13)**

## Messaging & Positioning

### Core Value Proposition
"Save 30+ minutes every night on documentation. Focus on your patients, not paperwork."

### Key Messages
1. **Time Savings:** 30 min/night × $150/hour = $75 value per night
2. **Burnout Reduction:** Documentation is #1 cause of therapist burnout
3. **Better Notes:** AI helps catch details you might miss
4. **HIPAA Compliance:** Enterprise-grade security built-in
5. **Easy Integration:** Works with your existing EHR

### Tone
- **Professional but approachable** (understand therapist's world)
- **Evidence-based** (cite research, show numbers)
- **Empathetic** (acknowledge burnout struggle)
- **Action-oriented** (clear benefits, easy to start)

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) & Lifetime Value (LTV)

### Assumptions
- **Trial-to-paid conversion:** 20-30% (high because problem is severe)
- **Customer lifetime:** 24+ months (sticky, high switching cost)
- **Churn:** 5%/month (low because integrated with daily workflow)
- **LTV:** $99/month × 19 months (average lifetime) = $1,881

### CAC Budget (Sustainable)
- **Target CAC:** <$300 (LTV/6 = $1,881/6)
- **Product Hunt:** $0 CAC (organic)
- **Email:** $0 CAC (organic from landing page)
- **Referrals:** $20 incentive per customer
- **Paid ads:** $50-100/month budget (LinkedIn, Google)

### Expected CAC Mix (Month 3)
| Channel | % of Users | CAC |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| Product Hunt | 30% | $0 |
| Referrals | 25% | $20 |
| Email/organic | 25% | $0 |
| Therapy forums | 15% | $0 |
| Paid ads | 5% | $100 |
| **Blended** | **100%** | **$20** |
